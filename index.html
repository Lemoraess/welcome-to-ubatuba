Ubatuba, the dazzling emerald coast of Brazil

Praia do Felix

Praia do Felix ‚Äì A secluded paradise with calm, crystal-clear waters, perfect for snorkeling and relaxing. The lush green hills surrounding the beach make it one of the most scenic spots in Ubatuba.

Praia da Almada

A family-friendly beach with gentle waves, golden sand, and great infrastructure (restaurants and beach kiosks). Ideal for swimming and stand-up paddleboarding.

What I like about it

Another perfect beach to go with the family and have a relaxing day. This beach has greater infrastructure with kiosks and restaurants

Praia do Prumirim

A breathtaking beach with a small island you can swim or kayak to at low tide. The waterfall (Cachoeira do Prumirim) nearby adds an extra touch of adventure!

What I like about it

With crystal clear waters and a breathtaking view, being on Prumirim beach, or heading to the island, is like being in paradise. I highly recommend it.
